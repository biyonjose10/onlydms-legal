OnlyDMs — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

OnlyDMs ("the app", "we", "us") is an Android app that helps you stay focused on Instagram direct messages by overlaying the non-DM parts of Instagram (Reels, Posts, Stories, Explore). This policy explains exactly what the app accesses and what — if anything — leaves your device. The short version: OnlyDMs does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or your message contents. Everything the app reads is processed on your device, in memory, and is never sent to us or anyone else.

1. Accessibility Service — what it accesses

OnlyDMs uses Android's Accessibility Service. This is required for the app's only function: detecting which Instagram screen is currently open so it can decide whether to show its overlay.

  • The service reads on-screen UI information from Instagram only (for example, identifiers of the screen currently displayed) to tell DMs apart from Reels/Posts/Stories.
  • This processing happens entirely on your device and only in memory. The app does not record, save to disk, log, upload, or share your screen contents, your messages, your contacts, or any other personal information.
  • The app never reads any application other than Instagram, and performs no action other than showing or hiding its own overlay and navigating you to your DMs.

2. Information collected through advertising (Google AdMob)

OnlyDMs is free and shows a single rewarded video ad when you choose to turn the blocker off, using Google AdMob. AdMob (a Google service) may collect and process your device's Advertising ID and related device information to serve and measure ads. This data is handled by Google, not by us. For details see Google's Advertising and Privacy Policy, and How Google uses information from AdMob. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID at any time in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

3. Information we do NOT collect

We do not operate any servers and have no accounts or sign-in. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, Instagram messages, or screen contents. The app's only stored data is your local settings (whether the blocker is on, and your in-app preferences), kept in private app storage on your device and never transmitted.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell or share your data. The only third party that receives any data is Google AdMob, as described in section 2, for the purpose of showing ads.

5. Data retention & security

Local settings remain on your device until you clear the app's data or uninstall it. Network traffic to AdMob is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Because the app keeps no personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on a server.

6. Children

OnlyDMs is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your choices

You can disable the Accessibility Service at any time in Android Settings → Accessibility → OnlyDMs, reset your Advertising ID in Android settings, or uninstall the app to remove all local data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
